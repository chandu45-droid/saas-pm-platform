# Competitor Pricing Research — Raw Data

## ClickUp (2026)
- **Free**: $0 — unlimited users, unlimited tasks, 100MB storage
- **Unlimited**: $7/user/mo (annual) / $10/user/mo (monthly)
- **Business**: $12/user/mo (annual) / $19/user/mo (monthly)
- **Business Plus**: ~$15–22/user/mo
- **Enterprise**: ~$25–40/user/mo (custom)
- **AI Add-on**: ClickUp Brain — $9/member/mo extra
- **Model**: Per-seat + feature-gated tiers

## Jira (2026)
- **Free**: $0 — up to 10 users, 2GB storage, 100 automation runs/mo
- **Standard**: ~$7.53–7.91/user/mo
- **Premium**: ~$14.54/user/mo (AI features, cross-team deps)
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing
- **Data Center**: $51,000/yr for 500 users (rising to $59,000 in Feb 2026)
- **Model**: Per-seat, progressive pricing (cost/user drops at scale)

## Asana (2026)
- **Personal**: $0 — 2-user max (post Nov 2025)
- **Starter**: $10.99/user/mo (annual) / $13.49 (monthly)
- **Advanced**: $24.99/user/mo (annual) / $30.49 (monthly)
- **Enterprise**: ~$35/user/mo (custom)
- **Enterprise+**: ~$45/user/mo (custom)
- **Quirk**: Seats sold in 5-seat increments above 5 users (6 users = pay for 10)
- **Model**: Per-seat + feature tiers + forced seat increments

## Monday.com (2026)
- **Free**: $0 — 2 seats, 3 boards, 500MB storage
- **Basic**: $9/seat/mo (annual) — unlimited boards, 5GB storage
- **Standard**: $12/seat/mo (annual) — timeline/Gantt, automations
- **Pro**: $19/seat/mo (annual) — private boards, formulas
- **Enterprise**: ~$24–30+/seat/mo (custom)
- **Quirk**: 3-seat minimum on all paid plans
- **Model**: Per-seat + feature tiers + seat minimums

## Notion (2026)
- **Free**: $0 — unlimited pages, 5MB upload cap, 7-day history
- **Plus**: $10/member/mo (annual) — unlimited blocks/uploads
- **Business**: $20/member/mo (annual) — AI bundled in, 90-day history
- **Enterprise**: ~$25–30+/member/mo (custom)
- **Model**: Per-member + feature tiers, AI bundled in higher tiers

## Zoho Projects (2026)
- **Free**: $0 — up to 5 users, 3 projects
- **Premium**: $4/user/mo — resource utilization, Blueprint automation
- **Enterprise**: $9/user/mo — custom roles, Gantt, portfolio dashboard
- **Ultimate**: $14/user/mo — higher limits on automations/reports
- **Model**: Per-user + feature tiers (cheapest of the bunch)

## Key Observations
1. Every single competitor uses per-seat pricing
2. Feature gating is universal — advanced features locked behind higher tiers
3. AI is the new upsell lever (ClickUp $9/mo add-on, Notion bundles into Business+)
4. Seat minimums/increments punish small teams (Monday 3-seat min, Asana 5-seat increments)
5. Enterprise pricing is opaque and negotiable everywhere
6. Zoho is the price leader at $4/user but has smallest feature set
7. Total cost for 50-person team ranges from $200/mo (Zoho Premium) to $2,250/mo (Asana Enterprise+)
