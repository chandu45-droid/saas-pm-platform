# SaaS PM Platform — Product Research Brief

## Product Vision

Build a project and task management platform where:
* All features are available to every customer.
* No feature-gated pricing tiers (Basic, Pro, Enterprise).
* No per-user/seat pricing.
* Customers pay only for the resources they actually consume.
* Pricing is transparent, fair, and significantly lower than competitors.

Examples:
* Unlimited users and unlimited features.
* Meter and charge only for resource-intensive usage such as:
  * Storage consumed
  * AI features
  * Automations executed
  * API requests
  * File uploads
  * Third-party integrations
  * Any other infrastructure-heavy actions

## Research Objectives

### 1. Market Analysis
* Analyze ClickUp, Jira, Asana, Monday.com, Notion, and Zoho Projects.
* Explain their pricing strategies.
* Explain why they charge per user and use feature tiers.
* Identify customer pain points with existing pricing models.
* Identify opportunities for a usage-based alternative.

### 2. Cost Structure Analysis
Break down the actual costs involved in operating a task management SaaS platform:

**Infrastructure:** Application servers, Databases, Storage, Backups, CDN and bandwidth, Monitoring and logging

**Platform Services:** Authentication, Email notifications, Push notifications, Search and indexing, Integration services

**Operations:** Customer support, Security and compliance, Maintenance and DevOps

Provide estimated costs for: 100 users, 1,000 users, 10,000 users, 100,000 users

Estimate: Monthly infrastructure cost, Approximate cost per active user, How costs scale with usage

### 3. Usage-Based Pricing Feasibility
Determine:
* Which actions genuinely increase operating costs
* Which actions cost almost nothing
* Which metrics are suitable for metered billing
* Which metrics customers would perceive as fair

Evaluate models: Pure pay-as-you-go, Base fee + usage, Workspace pricing + metered resources, Credits-based pricing

### 4. Unit Economics
Model hypothetical scenarios:

**Example A:** $10/workspace/month, Unlimited users, 5 GB storage included
**Example B:** $20/workspace/month, Unlimited users, Metered AI and automations

Calculate: Revenue, Gross margins, Break-even points, Infrastructure costs, Sustainability at different customer scales

### 5. Competitive Advantage
* Is there a real market gap?
* Would customers switch because of this pricing?
* Which customer segments would benefit the most?
* What are the biggest risks?

### 6. Founder Feasibility
Assume: AI-assisted development (Claude agents), lean team, minimal employees.

Estimate: MVP development requirements, Infrastructure requirements, Monthly operating expenses, Realistic timeline to launch, Team size for MVP → 100 customers → 1,000 customers
