## Pricing Model Comparison

| Aspect | Competitors | Our Platform |
|---|---|---|
| Pricing unit | Per seat/user/member | Per workspace |
| Feature access | Tiered (gated) | All features included |
| User limit | Depends on plan/tier | Unlimited |
| AI features | Add-on or high-tier only | Available to all, metered |
| Storage | Varies by tier (100MB–unlimited) | Metered (pay for what you use) |
| Automations | Limited by tier | Unlimited, metered |
| Billing transparency | Opaque enterprise pricing | Real-time usage dashboard |
| Seat minimums | Yes (Monday: 3, Asana: 2+5 increments) | None — unlimited users |

## Key Competitor Weaknesses to Exploit

1. **Asana**: 5-seat increments (6 users = pay for 10) — most punitive
2. **Monday.com**: 3-seat minimum on all paid plans — anti-small-team
3. **ClickUp**: AI as separate $9/mo add-on — nickel-and-diming
4. **Jira**: Complex progressive pricing — confusing at scale
5. **Notion**: Free tier gutted (2-user limit post Nov 2025) — community backlash
6. **Zoho**: Cheap but weak UX — opportunity to match price and beat experience
